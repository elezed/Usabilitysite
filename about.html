About us


Professional since 2001

Established in 2011, Wedding Plan is a boutique event planning company founded by Luigi Rossi and based in Pisa. Luigi Rossi has planned intimate affairs for 40 guests to more elaborate weddings for 300 guests. He has worked at venues such as the New York Public Library, The Plaza, Gotham Hall, Blue Hill at Stone Barns, The Foundry, The Bowery Hotel, Harold Pratt House, Cipriani and Wave Hill. Wedding Plan has been named a top wedding planner by Martha Stewart Weddings, one of the best classic NYC wedding planners by Brides, is a proud member of New York Magazine Weddings editor's pick of event planners, Style Me Pretty's Little Black Book and a recipient of Wedding Wire Couples' Choice Awards 2012-2014. Luigi Rossi's work has been featured in Martha Stewart Weddings, The Knot, Style Me Pretty, Snippet & Ink, Elizabeth Anne Designs, Once Wed, The Bride's Cafe, Merci New York and Munaluchi Bride.

With a focus on client service, he aims to provide personalized attention throughout the planning process. He will use his keen organizational skills and calm demeanor to make sure everything is executed flawlessly. He wants to help you to create a truly memorable event. Luigi Rossi loves the personal relationships she forms with her clients. A wedding is such a joyful and emotional celebration. The privilege of helping couples plan their weddings is something she finds very meaningful and rewarding.

Luigi Rossi has a diverse background, having lived in four countries over three continents. His passion for travelling and different cultures are tools she uses in planning unique events for her clients. He enjoys helping couples personalize their weddings with thoughtful details and has worked with many multi-cultural couples.
